Family Law and Adoption

Each family law matter involves a set of circumstances, facts, and personal history unique to the client. It encompass divorce, custody, and adoption etc. An adoption order is issued by the High Court in Cameroon. This is similar to other countries of the region. An adoption order transfers a child from one family to another by transferring parental responsibilities. After then, the child is treated for almost all legal reasons as if they were the adopters' legal children.

FDI/FPI Management

Capital is a vital ingredient for economic growth A foreign direct investment (FDI) is an investment made by a firm or individual in one country into business interests located in another country. Foreign portfolio investment (FPI) instead refers to investments made in securities and other financial assets issued in another country. Both methods of foreign investment are crucial to global trade and development. We provide reciprocal investment opportunities between the global north and South.
