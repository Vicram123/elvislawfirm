Welcome To E.N. Bongam and Partners Law Firm

E. N. LAW FIRM is full service and bijural, with a global perspective in innovative business investment and growth. We thrive to think ahead of the times in which we live and confidently bring original solutions to our clients' most complex legal and commercial challenges, help in the expansion into new markets and override the evermore complicated legal framework.

By harnessing our devoted individual and collective local knowledge, we provide pragmatic legal services to our clients globally. At a time when the complex regional and world business environment is getting increasingly sophisticated, we remain defiantly dedicated to address the cumulative and specific needs of our clients, through strategic counselling, efficient and innovative solutions to complex challenges.

Civil Law Dispute And Consultancy

Civil law is the counterpart to criminal law in Anglophone Cameroon and other common law oriented legal systems. It relates to matters and disputes between moral and phisical legal persons.

Criminal Law Dispute

Being accused of a criminal offence is an experience that can be devastating, not only for you, but for your family and loved ones. Expert legal advice, whether at a police station, in court, or our office is paramount in assisting you through what can be one of the most anxious situations you will experience.
